Hello! I'm Devin, web developer and Oregonian. I love digging into the nitty-gritty logistics of what makes things tick, and often find myself fixated on the organization and flow of data through a system. All the better if that system is user-interactive.

When earning my BS of Computer Science, I primarily coded in C, but I've since swapped my focus to JavaScript. I recently finished Thinkful's Engineering Immersion Program, a rigorous, five-month endeavor to learn full-stack web development. My main technologies are React-Redux on the front end, and Node/Express with MongoDB on the backend.

In my spare time, I'm an avid player of videogames (especially labor-of-love indie games), and have regularly played Dungeons & Dragons since 2012. Periodically, I also engage in collaborative worldbuilding and storytelling exercises with friends, creating lore and history for strange fantasy worlds.

Savage Planner

(React, Redux, Passport, JWT, MongoDB)

My first full-stack project!

I play tabletop games like Dungeons and Dragons frequently. One I had recently picked up at the time was the system Savage Worlds, so I decided to make a character creator/planner app for it.

This allows you to build fresh, 0XP Savage Worlds characters, then plan out your level-up choices. It also offers feedback if a given level-up choice is invalid. Registered users can make many characters, which are automatically saved for future editing/reference.
